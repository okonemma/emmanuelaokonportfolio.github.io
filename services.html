  • Boutique Collaborations

    One of my favourite things to do is shop, and I love supporting local boutiques! Honestly you guys rule, I'm so inspired by all of you establishing such beautifully curated shops.

    This package includes the following:

    - Meeting discussing vision behind the project

    - Instagram live or Instagram Stories in store

    - Creating outfits with clothing in store

    - Favorite pieces in store & review (via blog post or specific in Insta stories/live)

    - Blog post

    - Lookbook created with clothing from store + own wardrobe (with professional photographer)

    - All photos given to store for use + instagram content

    Packages starting at $250 - $1,250

    Package Cost + 5% GST + Travel Cost

    *Minimum of 2 hours

I have worked with clothing and Jewelry brands like ShoeDazzle, Converse, Shein, Dazeboutique etc. Honestly you guys rule, I'm so inspired by all of you establishing such beautifully curated online stores.
